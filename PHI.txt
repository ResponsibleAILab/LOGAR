PHI List (Clean version)
* Patient Full Name
* DOB
* Medical Record Number (MRN)
* Admission date
* Discharge date
* Age
* Gender
* Race/ethnicity
* Patient’s chief complaint / reason for visit
* History of present illness (HPI) – onset, duration, severity, triggers
* Observations (“what do you see happening during the encounter?”
* Lab results (if relevant)
* Past medical history
* Past psychiatric history
* Surgical history (if applicable)
* Problem list (active and resolved conditions)
* Medication list (include dosages, purpose, adherence, side effects)
* Social history (housing, occupation, supports, habits, stressors)
* Family history (medical and psychiatric)
* Patient’s perspective/priorities
* Immunization records
* Mental status examination (appearance, speech, mood, thought process, cognition, insight, etc. if psychiatric)
* Vital signs and lab results (if relevant)
* Key abnormal test findings
* Diagnostic list
* Differential diagnosis
* Diagnostic rationale
* Prognosis – likely course, expected outcomes, side effects of treatment
* Treatment plan (medications, therapy, procedures)
* Treatment plan rationale
* Follow-up care instructions
* Referrals to other providers or interdisciplinary contributions (nursing, pharmacy, social work, etc.)
* Progress notes (documentation of patient’s status across encounters)
